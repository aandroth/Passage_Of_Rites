Gameplay is three rites of passage where kobold youth become adults

0. Trap Making - Grab three ingredients to make a wacky trap
			   - Kobold cave workshop

Features:
	Stealing other player items
	Grabbing item from Supply
	Grabbed item refreshes
	Each player is randomly assigned trap to make
	Three items for each trap
	Loop is player gets items from supply tables, and brings it to their table for completing traps
	Most traps made is the winner



1. Rat Catch - Lots of rats are running around the front entrance. Catch as many as you can before time runs out.
			 - Entrance of the alchemist lair

2. Flame Dodge - Move across the hallway while not getting hit by the flame/projectiles that shoot out.
			   - Main hallway of the dungeon

3. Alchemist Golem Boss Battle - Whack the golem with a stick and then make your mark on the wall.
							   - Outside the door to the laboratory
